Introduction
What We Do.
Objective

E-Cell, MGMCET is dedicated to fostering an entrepreneurial spirit among students and cultivating their creativity and innovation. We believe that every great idea begins with a spark, and our mission is to nurture that spark into a transformative venture. By providing mentorship, networking opportunities, access to incubators, and guidance from industry experts, we aim to bridge the gap between conceptual ideas and real-world implementation.
We organize workshops, competitions, and interactive sessions that expose students to the entrepreneurial ecosystem, helping them understand business planning, market research, and funding strategies. Our goal is to create a thriving culture of innovation where students are empowered to take calculated risks, explore new technologies, and convert their ideas into impactful startups that can contribute meaningfully to society.
At MGMCET E-Cell, we don窶冲 just teach entrepreneurship窶背e live it, and we inspire students to do the same, preparing them to become the next generation of innovators, leaders, and changemakers.

E-Cell, MGMCET, organizes a wide range of events throughout the year to promote entrepreneurship within and beyond the campus. Our flagship event, the E-Summit, brings together students, innovators, and industry leaders from across India, creating a platform for networking and idea exchange. Initiatives like TEDx and StartUp Master Classes take learning beyond classrooms, inspiring students with real-world insights.
We also host lectures, workshops, and mentoring sessions aimed at nurturing budding entrepreneurs. Programs like the StartUp Internship Programme provide students with hands-on experience in startups, allowing them to understand operations, challenges, and growth strategies in depth. Through these activities, MGMCET E-Cell empowers students to transform innovative ideas into successful ventures.

  • Organize periodic workshops/seminars/ interactions with entrepreneurs, investors, professionals and create a mentor pool for student innovators.
  • Network with peers and national entrepreneurship development organizations.

Team Revel

Team Revel is the heart of MGMCET E-Cell, dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and creativity among students. We are a group of passionate, driven individuals who believe in turning ideas into impactful solutions. Through our initiatives, we aim to create an ecosystem where aspiring entrepreneurs can learn, experiment, and succeed.
From organizing E-Summits, TEDx events, and startup workshops to mentoring budding startups, we ensure every student has the guidance and resources they need to bring their vision to life. Our team collaborates with industry experts, alumni, and successful entrepreneurs to provide real-world insights and opportunities to our campus community.
At Team Revel, we believe that innovation thrives in a collaborative environment. We encourage students to think beyond boundaries, challenge the status quo, and embrace a mindset of problem-solving. Every project we undertake, every event we organize, and every workshop we conduct is aimed at empowering young minds to step confidently into the world of entrepreneurship.
